Business Communications

Assignment: User Needs Analysis

ITEC 242 Class Artifact

Artifact Description

Summary of the Artifact

This project involved analyzing the needs of a hypothetical business client and outlining our findings and recommendations.

Concepts, Theories, or Frameworks Applied
  • User-Centered Design (UCD): Prioritized end-user needs and pain points during analysis.
  • SWOT Analysis: Evaluated the client's strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats.
  • Cost-Benefit Analysis: Compared solutions based on budget and long-term value.
  • Stakeholder Communication: Tailored messaging for different audiences.
Skills/Competencies Demonstrated
  • Technical research
  • Team collaboration and task delegation
  • Persuasive communication
  • Professional visual slide design
Reflection

This project taught me to bridge technical solutions with business objectives through evidence-based recommendations. The experience mirrors real-world IT consulting, where professionals must justify costs and communicate complex ideas simply. For example, explaining cloud migration tools required translating technical features (e.g., encryption) into business benefits (e.g., cost savings). This skill is critical for IT roles where clear communication drives decision-making.
